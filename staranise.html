Star Anise
Star Anise is quarterly magazine celebrating Chinese American culture in New York. Each issue focuses on a single overarching topic spanning art, music, culture, and food. Star Anise seeks to explore cultural themes within second generation Chinese American lifestyles with a light hearted but critical touch.
