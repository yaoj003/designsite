Zest
Concept for a contemporary Asian Jazz festival at MOCA Geffen in Los Angeles.
Zest is a celebration of life and music, energy and immersion, East and West. This is a concept for a one day festival showcases Asian jazz artists at the MOCA Geffen Contemporary in Los Angeles. Zest was designed in three parts: the identity, the website for tickets sales, and the application for the day of the festival. The design is grid-based, using strong typographical elements and limited imagery.
