Brief: Why are so many period tracking apps pink? Create an app that celebrates women's health without falling into these common design tropes.
With Luna, get women’s healthcare on your schedule. Created for women by women, Luna celebrates women from all walks of life. Our free app offers period tracking, sex ed, fertility planning and more. Get birth control, UTI medication, and prescription skincare through virtual doctor’s appointments and delivered to your home. For all your women’s health needs, Luna’s got you.
