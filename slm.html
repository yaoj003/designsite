Sous Le Manteau
A luxury fragrance collection rooted in enchantment and seduction.
Created under the creative director Olivia Bransbourg, Sous Le Manteau is inspired by aphrodisiac potions from the Middle Ages, re-contextualized into a modern day love story focusing on enchantment and seduction. I created the logo and brand identity, expressing these in a website, as well as bottles and packaging for its line of fragrances, including a leaflet and ceramic pendant included inside. The typeface is inspired by medieval manuscripts, while the two lovers are created from photographs of Parisian caryatids.
