Bumblebee
Loungewear dedicated to the great female authors who nurtured our love of literature.
Bumblebee Loungewear is inspired by those lazy days at home, spent barefoot soaking in the afternoon sun. The apparel is soft and luxurious, beautiful and romantic, without sacrificing comfort or utility. Bumblebee is for the modern romantic, the young woman who balances her hectic life with a lazy afternoon and a good book.
